The Art Of War has been considered the definitive text on military strategy and warfare ever since ancient China, inspiring businesses, athletes, and of course generals to compete in the right way until today.

The Art Of War Quotesclose
  1. Appear weak when you are strong, and strong when you are weak.
  2. Victorious warriors win first and then go to war, while defeated warriors go to war first and then seek to win.
  3. In the midst of chaos, there is also opportunity.
  4. When you surround an army, leave an outlet free. Do not press a desperate foe too hard.
  5. The wise warrior avoids the battle.
  6. If you know the enemy and know yourself, your victory will not stand in doubt; if you know Heaven and know Earth, you may make your victory complete.
  7. The supreme art of war is to subdue the enemy without fighting.
  8. The greatest victory is that which requires no battle.
